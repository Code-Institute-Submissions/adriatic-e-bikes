Explore the 10 most beatiful Islands of Croatia with e-bikes!

Croatia
is rich in beautiful islands that attract an increasing number of tourists every year. But do you know how many Croatian islands there really are? To help you with this, we bring you names and the exact number of islands in Croatia. According to the latest data, there are currently 1244 large and slightly smaller islands in Croatia. Of these large numbers, not all are inhabited, there are only 67 of them. The largest islands in Croatia are: Cres, Krk, Brac, Hvar, Pag, Korcula, Dugi otok, Mljet, Vis and Rab.
Cres
 is the largest Croatian island.
