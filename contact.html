About Us

We are a Croatian company born with the idea that everyone can enjoy cycling. An e-bike is something that can afford us. You are not a professional driver, you are not an athlete, and you want to put yourself in the role of them sometimes. You want to take a bike ride to the top of a hill from one of the beautiful Croatian islands, with us you can do it. Remember our main goal is to provide with the best quality service for our riders so they can have a memorable experience.
